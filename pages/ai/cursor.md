# Cursor

## Features

### Plan Mode

For bigger tasks. Describe what you want to build, and Cursor writes a step-by-step plan you can approve or edit before it starts building.
Press `Shift+Tab` to try Plan Mode on a complex feature, refactor, or multi-file change in your backlog.

**Use the best models for plan (but they expensive)**:

1. claude-4.6-opus-high
1. composer-2-fast

**Use cheaper models for implementation**
