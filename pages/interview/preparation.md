# Before interview

1. Learn more about company at:
    * [Glassdoor](https://www.glassdoor.com/Reviews/Ezoic-Reviews-E672932.htm?filter.iso3Language=eng)
    * LinkedIn
    * Stackoverflow
    * Indeed
1. Evaluate:
    1. Work/Life balance
    1. Salary
    1. Health insurance
    1. 401K match
1. Estimate salary for your role/position
